Obsessed with

I'm a 4th year CSE undergraduate at Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology (AUST), Dhaka, with a deep passion for building things — from software systems to interactive game experiences.

I believe video games are the modern form of art: a culmination of storytelling, music, and visual media. This belief drives me to develop skills across the entire creative stack — programming, pixel art, music composition, and narrative writing.
